Origins

Xinjiang sits on the ancient Silk Road and nurtures a vibrant musical mosaic. Our aim is to preserve and share these priceless sounds through modern, open tools.

How We Work (lite)

  • Fieldwork: interview bearers, capture performances and oral histories.
  • Scores & DB: parallel staff/numbered notation; a searchable repository.
  • Open access: curated audio/video, instrument photos, teaching snippets.
